What is Ludo?

Ludo is a board game that originated in India and is now played around the world. It is a game of chance and strategy that can be played by two to four players.

About Ludo

Ludo is a game that has been played for centuries. It is a simple game that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. The game is played on a board with four different colored pieces for each player. The objective of the game is to be the first player to move all of their pieces from the starting point to the finish line.

Rules of Ludo

The rules of Ludo are straightforward. Each player takes turns rolling a dice and moving their pieces. Players can knock out each other's pieces if they land on the same spot, and players must move all their pieces to the finish line to win the game.

Variations of Ludo

Ludo has many variations, including different board sizes and number of players. Some variations also include additional rules, such as the ability to split a piece to move it more efficiently.
